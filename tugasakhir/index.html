Introduction

Categories

This website will give you more references for your home design. We will give some examples of designs that are most people's favorites. the first is a minimalist design, this design is the most popular interior design among modern people who have small houses and want a simple style. Minimalist home interior design is identical to neutral colors such as white, gray, and black. In addition to minimalism, modern home interior design is also often chosen by modern society. The elements used in modern home design can also be said to be similar to minimalist home interior designs. It's just that the interior design of this modern house is identical to furniture that uses iron and glass materials to create a shiny and modern feel. Next is contemporary design, this design continues to evolve from time to time. However, basically contemporary interior design is dominated by sleek and contrasting lines. In addition, lighting techniques are also used to focus on the accent wall.
